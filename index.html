Global Synergetic Foundation (GSFN)

Independent research and scholarly publishing initiative · New Delhi, India

Institutional Overview

The Global Synergetic Foundation (GSFN) — also referred to as GSFN, GSF Network and GSFN Bharat — is an independent interdisciplinary research and publishing initiative established in 1992, restructured in 2007, 2014 and 2024 in New Delhi, India. The foundation provides an intellectual platform supporting long-term scholarship examining structural relationships between classical knowledge traditions and contemporary scientific inquiry.

GSFN encourages research that integrates philosophical analysis, textual interpretation, and formal reasoning to address foundational questions concerning knowledge, cognition, cosmology, and the conceptual structure of scientific thought.

Institutional Purpose

The foundation supports independent scholarship bridging classical intellectual traditions and modern theoretical sciences through interdisciplinary research combining philosophy, textual studies, mathematics, and cognitive inquiry.

Publishing and Scholarly Communication

GSFN disseminates research through the Astitva publication series, an interdisciplinary programme supporting monographs, micro‑monographs, and scholarly research communications.
