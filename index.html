Introduction

Academic Formation

  • 1981

    Bachelor of Science — First Class First in Institution
    University of Gorakhpur. Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Psychology, and Philosophy, including Symbolic Logic and both Indian and Western philosophical traditions.

  • 1983

    Master of Arts, Mathematical Economics and Econometrics — First in Order of Merit
    Banaras Hindu University (BHU), Varanasi. Recipient of the Wagle Gold Medal and the BHU Gold Medal.

  • 1991

    Doctor of Philosophy — Banaras Hindu University
    Doctoral research across Mathematical Control Systems, the Economics of Information, and Psycho-physiological Behavioural Science, with particular focus on decision-making and organisational structures.

Research Orientation

His research investigates conceptual structures embedded in Vedic knowledge traditions and explores their philosophical significance for contemporary discussions in mathematics, cognition, cosmology, and philosophy of science.
