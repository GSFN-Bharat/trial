About

Dr. Sati Shankar is an Indian scholar and founder of the Global Synergetic Foundation (GSFN), engaged in long-term research on the foundations of knowledge, causality, and cosmology across Vedic and modern scientific traditions.

His work is centered on the Ṛg Veda and Brāhmaṇa literature as systematic explorations of Ṛta, Agni, and the structure of reality, tracing their continuity into classical darśanas and contemporary scientific thought.

Trained in mathematical economics and econometrics, his research integrates formal systems, decision theory, and cognitive science with Vedic epistemology, treating mathematics and śāstra as parallel languages of structure and causation.

A central axis of his work is the investigation of mind (manas), order (ṛta), and energy (agni) as unified principles across cosmology, cognition, and ontology.
